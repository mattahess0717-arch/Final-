Return & Refund Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

At Cashflow Challenge (“Company,” “we,” “us,” or “our”), we provide digital products, software access, and online training programs through cashflowchallenge.ai (“Site”). This Return & Refund Policy explains how payments, cancellations, and trial access work when you enroll in our programs.

By purchasing or accessing our services, you agree to this policy.

1. No Refunds — Digital Access

All purchases, including:

  • Software access
  • Online training
  • Digital resources
  • Subscription fees

…are final and non-refundable.

Once access is granted, we cannot reverse delivery of digital content or tools. This includes all trial payments, upgrades, and recurring charges.

2. $1 Trial Policy

We may offer a $1 promotional trial for new users.

During the trial:

  • You receive full or limited access depending on the offer
  • The trial lasts 14 days unless stated otherwise
  • Unless cancelled before the trial ends, the regular subscription price will begin automatically on the next billing date

No refunds are provided for trial charges, or for failure to cancel before renewal.

3. Subscription Cancellation

You may cancel future billing at any time by:

  • Logging into your account, or
  • Contacting us at info@cashflowchallenge.ai

Cancellations only affect future billing — charges already processed are final and non-refundable.

Your access will remain active until the end of the current paid period.

4. Payment Disputes

Unauthorized charge disputes should be directed to your payment provider.
However, filing a chargeback for valid charges may result in:

  • Immediate account termination
  • Removal of system access
  • Loss of all data and progress

We appreciate the chance to resolve billing issues directly.

5. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated periodically. The “Last Updated” date will reflect the most recent version.
Ongoing use of the Site means you accept any changes.
